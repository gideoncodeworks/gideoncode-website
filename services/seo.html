Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see SEO results?

SEO is a long game. Most businesses start seeing meaningful ranking improvements within 3-6 months. Local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization can show results faster, sometimes within 4-8 weeks. We provide monthly reports so you can track progress.

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

AEO is optimization for AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity. When someone asks an AI assistant for a recommendation in your industry, AEO helps ensure your business gets cited. We add structured data, build authoritative content, and monitor AI citations.

Do I need a new website before starting SEO?

Not necessarily. If your current site is reasonably modern and mobile-friendly, we can optimize it. If your site is outdated, slow, or built on a platform that limits SEO, we may recommend a redesign first. We'll give you an honest assessment during our consultation.

What do you track and report on?

We track keyword rankings, organic traffic, Google Business Profile views and actions, local pack positions, click-through rates, and AI citations (on Pro plans). You get a clear monthly report with real numbers, not fluff.

Can I cancel my SEO plan?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. We recommend committing to at least 6 months to see meaningful results, but you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll never hold your site hostage.
