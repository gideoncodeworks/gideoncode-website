Frequently Asked Questions

How do Google Ads work for a masonry company in a market as spread out as Jacksonville?

+

We use hyper-targeted geographic settings to ensure your ads only show in the specific zip codes and neighborhoods where you want to work. For Jacksonville masonry companies, this is critical because a lead from Mandarin is very different from a lead in Fernandina Beach in terms of drive time, project type, and average job value. We create separate campaigns for different service areas so you can allocate more budget to the zones that generate your most profitable work. This geographic precision means every dollar goes toward reaching homeowners in your sweet spot, not people 45 minutes away across town.

What kind of masonry jobs can Google Ads help me land in Jacksonville?

+

We build campaigns targeting specific high-value services like stucco repair Jacksonville, retaining wall installation, brick mailbox construction, and concrete block fence building, which are among the most searched masonry terms in the Northeast Florida market. We also create campaigns for commercial masonry work targeting property managers and general contractors searching for subcontractors. Florida-specific services like seawall repair and hurricane damage restoration can be activated seasonally to capture surge demand. By targeting specific job types rather than generic masonry searches, we attract the leads most likely to convert into projects that are worth your crew's time.

How much should a Jacksonville masonry company budget for Google Ads?

+

We typically recommend starting with $1,500-3,000 per month in ad spend for a masonry company in the Jacksonville market, plus our management fee. Masonry keywords tend to have lower competition and cost-per-click than trades like plumbing and HVAC, often running $4-8 per click compared to $15-30 for emergency plumbing terms. This means your budget stretches further and you can generate a meaningful number of leads without a massive investment. A single retaining wall or patio project generating $5,000-15,000 in revenue more than justifies the monthly ad spend.

Can you help me get leads for both residential and commercial masonry work in Jacksonville?

+

Absolutely, and we keep them in separate campaigns because the messaging, keywords, and landing pages need to be completely different. Residential campaigns target homeowners searching for outdoor kitchen masonry, brick paver installation, and decorative stone work with imagery and language that appeals to property improvement motivations. Commercial campaigns target general contractors, property developers, and facility managers with messaging focused on capacity, licensing, timeline reliability, and portfolio of completed commercial projects. Running them as separate campaigns also lets you independently control budget allocation between residential and commercial based on which pipeline needs more leads at any given time.
