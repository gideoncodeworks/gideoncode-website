Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects launch within 2-3 weeks from the time we receive your content and branding. Complex sites with custom features may take 4-6 weeks. We keep you updated throughout the entire process.

Do I own my website?

Yes. You own your domain, your content, and your design. If you ever leave, we hand everything over. No hostage situations, no hidden fees to export your site.

What if I need changes after launch?

All of our plans include ongoing support. Text or email us your changes and we handle them, usually within 24-48 hours. No tickets, no waiting in a queue.

Do you do website redesigns?

Absolutely. If you have an existing site that looks outdated or isn't performing, we can redesign it from the ground up while preserving your SEO rankings and existing content.

What's included in hosting?

Our hosting includes SSL certificates, daily backups, uptime monitoring, security updates, and fast load times on modern infrastructure. It's all bundled into your monthly plan so there are no surprise bills.
