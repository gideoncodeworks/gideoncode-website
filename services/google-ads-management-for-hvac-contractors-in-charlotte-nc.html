Frequently Asked Questions

How much should an HVAC company in Charlotte spend on Google Ads?

+

Most HVAC contractors in the Charlotte metro see strong results with a monthly ad spend between $3,000 and $6,000, plus our management fee. The exact budget depends on your service area, the types of jobs you want to attract, and how aggressively your competitors are bidding. We recommend increasing spend by 30-50% during peak demand months like July and August when cost-per-click rises but so does conversion value. Our goal is always to keep your cost per booked job under $150, which represents a strong return for HVAC companies doing $5,000-15,000 installations.

Can Google Ads help my HVAC company get more installation jobs instead of just repair calls?

+

Yes, and that is one of the biggest advantages of professionally managed campaigns over DIY efforts. We create separate campaigns targeting homeowners searching for AC replacement Charlotte, new furnace installation, and HVAC system upgrade, which are the higher-ticket jobs most contractors want. We also use audience targeting to reach homeowners in neighborhoods with aging housing stock in areas like Plaza Midwood and Dilworth where systems are more likely to need full replacement. This segmented approach ensures your budget is weighted toward the job types that generate the most revenue.

How do you prevent my HVAC ads from showing to people outside Charlotte?

+

We use precise geographic targeting at the zip code level to ensure your ads only show to people within your actual service area. This is critical in the Charlotte market because the metro spans parts of both North and South Carolina, and many HVAC companies waste significant budget on clicks from areas they do not serve. We also set up location exclusions and monitor search term reports weekly to block irrelevant geographic queries. If you only want to serve Mecklenburg and Cabarrus counties, your ads will only appear to searchers in those areas.

What kind of results can I expect in the first 90 days of Google Ads management?

+

In the first 30 days, we focus on campaign buildout, keyword research specific to the Charlotte HVAC market, and launching initial campaigns. By day 60, we typically have enough data to optimize bids, pause underperforming keywords, and double down on what is working. Most Charlotte HVAC clients see their cost per lead drop by 30-40% between month one and month three as we refine targeting. By the 90-day mark, you should have a clear picture of your average cost per booked job and a predictable pipeline of incoming service calls.
