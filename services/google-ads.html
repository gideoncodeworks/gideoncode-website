Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I spend on Google Ads?

We recommend starting with $500-$1,000 per month in ad spend, plus our $297 management fee. The right budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals. During our consultation, we'll research your market and give you a realistic recommendation.

How fast do Google Ads start working?

Google Ads can start generating leads within days of launching. However, it typically takes 2-4 weeks of data to properly optimize campaigns. The first month is about gathering data and refining targeting. By month 2-3, you should see strong, consistent results.

Do you write the ad copy?

Yes. We write all ad headlines, descriptions, and extensions. We also create and optimize landing pages if needed. Good ad copy is half the battle, and we test multiple variations to find what converts best for your business.

What if my ads aren't working?

We monitor campaigns daily and make adjustments based on real performance data. If something isn't working, we pause it, test alternatives, and reallocate budget to what is working. You're never locked in — you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
