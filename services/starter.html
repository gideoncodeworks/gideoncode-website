Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade to a higher plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Starter to Growth or Domination at any time. We'll migrate your existing pages and content seamlessly — no downtime, no rebuilding from scratch. You only pay the difference in monthly cost going forward.

What happens after my 24-month contract ends?

After your contract ends, you can continue on a month-to-month basis at the same rate, renew for another term, or cancel. Your website files and content are always yours — we never hold your site hostage.

Do I own my website?

Yes. You own all the content, images, and copy on your website. If you ever decide to leave, we'll provide all your files. The only thing we retain is our proprietary code and framework, but your content and data are 100% yours.

How fast can you build my website?

Most Starter websites are live within 2-3 weeks from the day we receive your content and deposit. If you need it faster, let us know — we can often accommodate rush timelines for an additional fee.
