The best time to start is at least 60-90 days before the summer rush, which means launching campaigns no later than March for the San Antonio market. SEO improvements take time to compound, and you want your organic rankings and ad campaigns fully optimized before the first 100-degree day sends thousands of homeowners to Google searching for AC repair. However, we work with HVAC companies year-round and can always find immediate opportunities, especially for heating-related campaigns in the winter months that most San Antonio HVAC marketers completely ignore. Starting in the off-season also means lower ad costs and less competition for your initial campaign launch.

We build automated systems that capture every lead even when your phones are overwhelmed, including online booking forms, automated text responses to missed calls, and email nurture sequences that keep potential customers engaged until you can schedule them. During peak periods, we can strategically adjust your ad campaigns to focus on higher-value jobs like system replacements and new installations rather than low-margin repair calls. We also help you set up a waitlist system that converts summer overflow leads into fall maintenance agreement signups. This approach ensures you are not just surviving the summer rush but building long-term customer relationships that generate revenue year-round.

For San Antonio HVAC companies, Google Ads and local SEO are the primary revenue drivers because they capture homeowners actively searching for service. Google Local Service Ads are particularly effective for HVAC because the Google Guaranteed badge builds instant trust with homeowners worried about hiring a reliable contractor. We layer in Facebook and Instagram retargeting to stay in front of homeowners who visited your site but did not call, which is especially effective during the consideration phase for big-ticket installations. Email marketing to your existing customer list is the most underutilized channel in the HVAC industry and typically delivers the highest ROI for maintenance agreement sales.

We track everything back to revenue, not vanity metrics like impressions or website visits. Every campaign includes call tracking with recording, form submission tracking, and cost-per-lead calculations broken down by service type and geographic area. We provide monthly reports showing exactly how many calls came from Google Ads versus organic search versus your Google Business Profile, and what percentage of those calls converted to booked jobs.
