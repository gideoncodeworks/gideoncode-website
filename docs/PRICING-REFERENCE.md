## Pricing Strategy Notes

### Contract Terms
- **One-Time Builds:** Single payment, includes 1 year hosting, support ends after initial period (30-90 days)
- **Web-as-a-Service:** Setup fee + monthly recurring, available in 12-month or 24-month contracts
- **24-Month Contracts:** Always marked as "BEST VALUE" with savings clearly displayed
- **Add-On Services:** Monthly recurring, can be added to any website plan

### Savings Calculations
- **Starter 24-month:** Save $900 ($37.50/mo savings × 24 months)
- **Growth 24-month:** Save $1,068 ($44.50/mo savings × 24 months)
- **Domination 24-month:** Save $1,200 ($50/mo savings × 24 months)

### Payment Terms
- **Deposits:** One-time builds require 50% deposit to start, balance due at launch (mentioned in FAQ)
- **Payment Methods:** Credit cards, ACH transfers, wire transfers accepted
- **Payment Plans:** Available for web apps and mobile apps over $5,000

### Support Included
- **One-Time Builds:** 30-90 days depending on tier
- **Web Apps:** 90 days post-launch
- **Mobile Apps:** 90 days post-launch
- **Web-as-a-Service:** Ongoing support included in monthly fee
