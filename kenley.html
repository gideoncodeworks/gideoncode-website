Why I Do This

For the past year and a half, I knocked on doors selling internet services. Every day, I had real conversations with real people about real problems. That experience taught me something important: people don't want to be sold—they want solutions that actually work.

I saw firsthand how many local businesses were struggling online. Great services, terrible websites. Or worse—no website at all. They were losing customers to competitors who simply showed up better on Google.

That's when I made the pivot. I joined Gideon Codeworks because I believe a professional website is the single most effective tool for growing a business today. It's your 24/7 salesperson, your first impression, and often the deciding factor for customers choosing between you and the competition.

My goal is simple: help Findlay and Northwest Ohio businesses establish an online presence that truly reflects their brand and drives real growth. No tech headaches, no confusing jargon—just a website that works as hard as you do.
