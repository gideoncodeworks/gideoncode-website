What Is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

The next evolution of search — and it's included in every SEO plan.

The Problem

More people are searching with AI instead of Google. When someone asks ChatGPT "best plumber in Phoenix" or "who builds websites for small businesses" — is your business in the answer? If not, you're invisible to the fastest-growing search channel.

What We Do About It

We structure your website so AI systems can understand your business, pull accurate information, and cite you as a source. Schema markup, Q&A content, structured data — all the signals that make ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI recommend you.

AEO isn't an upsell — it's just good SEO done right for 2026. It's included in both our SEO + AEO Basic and SEO + AEO Pro plans.
