GoodCattery.com

Verified Maine Coon breeder marketplace • Live • Revenue-generating

GoodCattery is a full-scale marketplace connecting verified cat breeders with buyers. It includes three-party payments, AI-powered features, electronic contracts, a complete admin operations center, and 26 SEO-optimized content pages — all built and maintained by Gideon Codeworks.

Stripe Connect Payments

Three-party payment system: buyer pays, platform holds, breeder receives. Full onboarding, KYC verification, webhook handling, refunds, and platform fee calculation.

AI Chatbot & Outreach

Claude-powered chatbot on every page with persistent history. Plus AI email generation that scrapes breeder websites and writes personalized outreach with tracking.

E-Signatures & Contracts

Custom contract templates, HTML generation, draw-to-sign canvas, status tracking, and escrow-style deposit holding with verification badges.

Admin Operations Center

Campaign manager, partner pipeline CRM, verification workflow, content moderation, support tickets, and user management — six tools in one dashboard.

SEO & Content Engine

26 long-form blog posts, 8 breed landing pages, structured data on every page type, dynamic sitemap generation, and AEO optimization for AI search.

Auth, Database & Notifications

Google OAuth + email auth, 25 database tables with row-level security, community forum, 28 automated email templates, and multi-business management.
