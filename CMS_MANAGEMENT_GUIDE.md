## üìù **Common Tasks**

### **1. Edit Text on a Page**

**WordPress:**
1. Click **Pages** in left sidebar
2. Find the page you want to edit (e.g., "About")
3. Click **Edit**
4. Find the text block you want to change
5. Click into it and type your new text
6. Click **Update** (top right)

**Sanity/Contentful:**
1. Click **Documents** or **Content**
2. Find your page
3. Click the field you want to edit
4. Type your new text
5. Click **Publish**

---

### **2. Change an Image**

**WordPress:**
1. Go to the page with the image
2. Click **Edit**
3. Click on the image
4. Click **Replace**
5. Upload new image
6. Click **Update**

**Sanity/Contentful:**
1. Open the document
2. Click the image field
3. Click **Upload** or **Select from library**
4. Choose new image
5. Click **Publish**

---

### **3. Add a New Blog Post**

**WordPress:**
1. Click **Posts** ‚Üí **Add New**
2. Enter title
3. Write your content
4. Add featured image (top right sidebar)
5. Click **Publish**

**Sanity/Contentful:**
1. Click **Create New** ‚Üí **Blog Post**
2. Fill in:
   - Title
   - Content
   - Featured image
   - Category
3. Click **Publish**

---

### **4. Update Hours, Phone, or Address**

**WordPress (with ACF/Custom Fields):**
1. Go to **Pages** ‚Üí **Contact** (or Footer Settings)
2. Find the **Contact Info** section
3. Update the fields
4. Click **Update**

**Sanity/Contentful:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Site Info**
2. Update contact fields
3. Click **Publish**

---

### **5. Add/Remove Products (E-commerce)**

**WooCommerce (WordPress):**
1. Click **Products** ‚Üí **All Products**
2. To add: Click **Add New**, fill in details, set price, upload images
3. To remove: Hover over product, click **Trash**
4. Click **Publish** or **Update**

**Shopify:**
1. Log into Shopify admin
2. Click **Products** ‚Üí **Add product**
3. Fill in title, description, price, images
4. Click **Save**

**Snipcart:**
1. Edit the page where product appears
2. Update product details in the Snipcart code block
3. Save and publish

## üõ°Ô∏è **Security Best Practices**

### **Keep Your Site Safe:**

1. **Use a strong password**
   - At least 12 characters
   - Mix of letters, numbers, symbols
   - Use a password manager (1Password, LastPass)

2. **Update plugins/themes regularly**
   - Check for updates monthly
   - Read update notes before applying
   - Backup before major updates (we do this automatically)

3. **Don't share your login**
   - Each user should have their own account
   - Remove old users when they leave

4. **Enable 2-Factor Authentication**
   - Install plugin: Wordfence or Google Authenticator
   - Adds extra login security

## üí° **Tips & Tricks**

### **1. Always Preview Before Publishing**
Click the **Preview** button to see how changes look before making them live.

### **2. Save Drafts Often**
Don't lose your work! Click **Save Draft** every few minutes.

### **3. Use Alt Text for Images**
Helps with SEO and accessibility:
1. Click image
2. Add description in "Alt Text" field
3. Save

### **4. Keep URLs Clean**
When creating new pages:
- Use lowercase
- Separate words with hyphens
- Keep it short
- Example: `yoursite.com/about-us` (good)
- Example: `yoursite.com/About%20Us%20Page` (bad)

### **5. Optimize Images Before Upload**
- Use JPG for photos
- Use PNG for logos/graphics
- Resize to 1200px wide max
- Compress with TinyPNG.com before uploading
