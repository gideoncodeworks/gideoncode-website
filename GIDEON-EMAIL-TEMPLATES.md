## BEST PRACTICES

### Timing
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm (best open rates)
- Follow up once after 3 days if no response
- Don't spam - max 2 emails per prospect

### Personalization
- Use their actual business name
- Reference specific pain points for their industry
- Mention previous work you've done for them

### Offer Structure
- Lead with value, not features
- Use social proof (demo on your site, other clients)
- Create urgency (limited slots, early bird pricing)
- Make next step easy ("Reply YES")

### Pricing Strategy
- Start with discount for existing clients ($199 vs $299)
- Time-limited offers work well
- Bundle setup fee into first month if needed to close

### Success Metrics to Track
- Email open rate (aim for 30%+)
- Response rate (aim for 15%+)
- Demo-to-close rate (aim for 50%+)
- Time to first paying customer (target: 1 week)
