Founder

Josh Stone

Cleveland, Ohio

I ran a construction company for years. Dealt with the same stuff you deal with — customers who don't call back, marketing that doesn't work, and "web guys" who charge a fortune and disappear.

When I needed a website, I got quoted $8,000. For a small business site. I thought that was insane, so I taught myself to code instead.

Turns out I was pretty good at it. Started building sites for friends. Then friends of friends. Then I realized — small businesses don't need a $10K website. They need something that works, looks professional, and doesn't break the bank.

That's Gideon Codeworks. Real websites for real businesses, built by someone who actually understands what you're going through.
