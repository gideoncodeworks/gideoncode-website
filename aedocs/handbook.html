1. About Gideon Codeworks

Who We Are

Gideon Codeworks is a web design and digital marketing agency based in Cleveland, Ohio, serving small businesses nationwide. We specialize in Website-as-a-Service (WaaS) - we build, host, and manage websites so business owners can focus on running their business.

What Makes Us Different

  • No disappearing act - We're a real company, not a freelancer who might vanish
  • Everything included - Hosting, updates, security, support - one monthly price
  • AI-powered - Every website includes a 24/7 AI chatbot at no extra charge
  • Month-to-month - After the initial term, clients can cancel anytime
  • Real support - Clients talk to humans, not bots

Our Mission

Help small businesses compete online with professional websites and digital marketing they can actually afford.

2. Your Role as an Account Executive

What You Do

  1. Find prospects - Identify small businesses that need our services
  2. Start conversations - Reach out and build relationships
  3. Understand needs - Learn what each business needs to succeed online
  4. Present solutions - Match our services to their problems
  5. Close deals - Get the signature and payment
  6. Hand off to production - We build it, you move to the next prospect

What You DON'T Do

Your Status: Independent Contractor

  • You set your own hours

  • You can work for other companies (just not direct competitors)
  • Your income is directly tied to your results

5. The Sales Process

Step 1: Prospecting

Where to find prospects:

  • Google "[city] + [business type]" - look for bad/no websites
  • Facebook local business groups
  • LinkedIn business owners
  • Referrals from current clients
  • Local networking events

Step 2: Outreach

Cold Call Script:
"Hi, is this [Name]? This is [Your Name] with Gideon Codeworks. I was looking at local [industry] businesses and noticed [observation about their online presence]. Do you have 30 seconds?"

Step 3: Discovery

Key questions:

  1. "Do you currently have a website?"
  2. "How are you getting customers right now?"
  3. "What's your biggest challenge with your online presence?"
  4. "Have you worked with a web company before?"
  5. "If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal website do?"

Step 4: Handling Objections

ObjectionResponse
"Too expensive""What's one new customer worth to you?"
"Need to think""What specifically? Maybe I can help clarify."
"I can use Wix""Do you want to spend weekends on your website?"
"My nephew can do it""Will they be there at 2am when it breaks?"

Step 5: Closing

Trial close: "Does this sound like what you're looking for?"

Assumptive close: "Great, let's get you set up. I just need a few details..."

10. Building Your Team

After you close 10 verified deals, you unlock the ability to recruit other AEs.

How Overrides Work

Example: Your recruit Sarah sells Growth WaaS + Social ($502.50/mo)

  • Sarah earns: $100.50/mo (her 20%)
  • You earn: $25.13/mo (your 5% override)
  • That's passive income from her work

Recruiting Tips

  • Focus on your own sales first
  • Look for people with sales experience or strong networks
  • Set clear expectations (100% commission)
  • Share your own results as proof

11. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long to close my first deal?
A: Most AEs close within 2-4 weeks. Focus on activity volume early on.

Q: Can I sell in any location?
A: Yes. No territory restrictions. We serve clients nationwide.

Q: Do I need to be technical?
A: No. Understand what we offer, but you don't need to build websites.

Q: Can I sell to friends and family?
A: Yes, but family member sales require prior approval.

Q: What if I want to take a break?
A: You're independent. Take time off whenever. Recurring commissions continue.
