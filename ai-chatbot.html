Pricing FAQs

What counts as a "conversation"?

One conversation = one visitor's complete chat session. If they ask 5 questions, that's still 1 conversation. Most businesses use 200-400/month.

What happens if I go over my limit?

We'll notify you at 80% usage. If you exceed, we charge $0.50/additional conversation (Professional plan) or can upgrade you to the next tier.

What's included in setup?

Persona development call, business knowledge training, brand voice customization, website integration, testing, and go-live support. Everything to get Gideon perfect for YOUR business.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel with 30 days notice. Setup fee is non-refundable after we begin training, but monthly billing stops when you cancel.
