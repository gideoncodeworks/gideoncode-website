Launching the Account Executive Program

Launch Your Freedom — The Account Executive Program

We're scaling financial freedom beyond ourselves.

If you're a closer who hates overhead, product risk, or waiting for permission to earn — this program was built for you.

The Gideon Codeworks Account Executive Program lets you build real, recurring income without quitting your day job. But if you're successful, stay consistent, and build your team — let's be honest — you will be quitting.

From cluttered chaos to clean digital selling — no overhead, no limits.

A New Kind of Sales Model

Traditional MLMs and sales gigs come with buy-ins, product storage, and pressure.
We stripped all that away.

At Gideon, there's no upfront investment, no inventory, and no territory limits. You bring relationships and hustle — we handle fulfillment, technology, and support.

You don't have to design, code, or manage clients after the sale. You just close deals, build relationships, and create a stream of recurring income that grows every month.

How It Scales

Start part-time. Keep your job. Build your foundation.

As your client base expands, your income compounds. Then, when you're ready, you can recruit and mentor other Account Executives — turning your personal effort into a self-sustaining business.

That's the turning point: when you've built a team, a system, and a legacy that earns with or without you.

Three simple stages: Start as an AE, Build a Team, Earn Freedom.

No gatekeepers. No buy-ins. Just opportunity.

Zero Barriers. Real Infrastructure.

Most "opportunities" make you buy a starter kit or stack products in your garage.
We give you infrastructure, automation, and mentorship from day one.

Every AE gets:

  • Gideon's AI-powered CRM and lead tools
  • Proven pitch decks, templates, and scripts
  • Real-time coaching and daily enablement
  • Full delivery and support handled by our internal team

You're not guessing. You're growing — with tools that already work.

Work on your terms. Grow anywhere. Lead your life.

The Mission

The AE Program was built to scale freedom — not hype.

We believe anyone with drive and discipline deserves a shot at recurring income, without the barriers or buy-ins. It's a way to grow like a founder without taking startup risks.

Your only investment is your time, effort, and consistency.
And if you put those in, your nine-to-five won't stand a chance.

Ready to Build Something Bigger?

You don't have to quit your job to start.
But if you put in the work and build your team, you'll be quitting soon enough — for the right reasons.

"You don't have to quit your job to start — but if you build it right, you'll want to." — Josh Stone, Founder
