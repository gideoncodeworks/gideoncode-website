Your Launch Timeline

1

Discovery Call (30 min)

We'll hop on a quick call to learn about your business. We'll ask about:

  • - What services you offer
  • - What areas you serve
  • - Who your ideal customers are
  • - Any competitors you like (or want to beat)
  • - Your business address and phone number

Your effort: One 30-minute phone call

2

We Build Everything (1-2 weeks)

While you run your business, we're working behind the scenes:

  • - Create your Google account and business email
  • - Set up your Google Business Profile
  • - Design and build your website
  • - Configure analytics and tracking
  • - Optimize for search engines

Your effort: Send us any photos you want on the site (optional - we can use professional stock photos)

3

Google Verification (5 minutes)

Google will mail a postcard to your business address with a 5-digit PIN. This usually takes 5-7 business days.

Why is this necessary?

Google requires business owners to verify their address to prevent fraud. This is the only step we legally cannot do for you. Once you receive the postcard, just call or text us the code - takes 30 seconds.

Your effort: Check your mail, read us a 5-digit code over the phone

4

Review & Launch

We'll send you a preview link to review your website. Request any changes, and once you approve, we go live!

Your effort: Review the site, tell us any changes, say "looks good!"

5

You're Live! Now What?

Your website is live, you're on Google Maps, and leads can find you. From here:

  • - Answer your phone when it rings
  • - Ask happy customers for Google reviews (we'll show you how)
  • - Send us updates anytime you want to change something on the site

We handle all the ongoing maintenance, security, and updates. You just run your business.

  • 3. Phone Number — the number you want customers to call

Optional (Nice to Have)

Don't have all this ready? No problem. We'll gather what we need during the discovery call.
