Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need sales experience?

Preferred but not required. If you have hustle, coachability, and can handle rejection, we'll train you on the rest.

When do I get paid?

Semi-monthly (twice per month). First payment comes 15 days after your first closed deal. All setup commissions and recurring commissions are included in one payment.

What if I can't hit those performance numbers?

There's no quota or minimum. The numbers we showed are "good performance" benchmarks. You can work at your own pace, but obviously the more you sell, the more you earn.

Do I have to recruit a team?

No. You can stay as a solo Account Executive forever if you want. Team building is optional but unlocks higher income through overrides.

Can I really do this part-time?

Yes. Plenty of reps work this around a day job. You won't hit full-time numbers, but you can absolutely build a solid side income.

What's the actual commission percentage?

We discuss exact percentages during the phone interview. It varies based on package type and whether you're earning setup commission, recurring commission, or team overrides.

Do I need to pay anything to get started?

No. This is not a franchise or business opportunity you buy into. We provide all tools, training, and CRM access at no cost.

What if I don't get accepted?

We hire broadly, but not everyone. If we're not a fit, we'll let you know quickly so you're not waiting around.
