Frequently Asked Questions

Fast clarity on how we work, when we launch, and what makes our model different.

General Questions

How fast can we launch?

Web-as-a-Service sites ship in 21 days or less with content, automations, and analytics ready. Need it sooner? We stack extra sprints at no extra cost.

What's included monthly?

Strategy, design, hosting, SEO, unlimited updates, CRO experiments, and a direct line to the builders actually doing the work.

Can we start with the CRM only?

Absolutely. Buy or rent the platform, plug in your team, and we'll optionally bolt on our Web-as-a-Service plans or sales recruiting support later.

What makes Gideon Code Works different?

We're not an agency—we're a digital infrastructure studio built on AI-leveraged execution. We move faster, build smarter, and deliver results that traditional agencies can't touch. Plus, every build has Gideon (dual Claude + GPT-4) watching for issues and optimizations.

Pricing & Plans

What's the difference between one-time builds and Web-as-a-Service?

One-time builds are $2,497-$4,997 with 1 year of hosting included. Web-as-a-Service starts with a smaller setup fee ($497-$997) and includes ongoing monthly management ($212-$297/mo), unlimited updates, SEO, and optimization. Think of Web-as-a-Service as having a full digital team on retainer.

Are there contracts or can I cancel anytime?

Web-as-a-Service plans have 12 or 24-month terms to ensure we can deliver real ROI. One-time builds have no ongoing commitment—just pay the project fee and you own the site.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers. Web-as-a-Service plans are billed monthly. One-time builds require a 50% deposit to start, with the balance due at launch.

Do you offer refunds?

We stand behind our work. If we don't deliver on our promises within the first 30 days, we'll make it right or refund your setup fee. Monthly service fees are non-refundable but you can cancel before your next billing cycle.

Process & Timeline

What do you need from me to get started?

Just your branding assets (logo, colors), any existing content you want to use, and answers to our onboarding questionnaire about your business goals. We handle the rest—strategy, design, copywriting, and technical implementation.

How involved do I need to be during the build?

We keep it lean. Expect 2-3 short review calls and async feedback via our project dashboard. We don't overwhelm you with meetings—we build, you approve, we launch.

What happens after launch?

For one-time builds: you get 1 year of hosting and basic support. For Web-as-a-Service: we continuously optimize, update content, monitor performance, and run conversion experiments. You have direct access to make update requests anytime.

Can you help with content and copywriting?

Yes! Our team includes experienced copywriters who specialize in conversion-focused content. We'll write everything from scratch or polish your existing content to make it revenue-focused.

Technical Questions

What platform do you build on?

We use modern web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and deploy to Netlify or Vercel for blazing-fast performance. For more complex needs, we build custom solutions with Next.js or other frameworks. Everything is optimized for speed, SEO, and conversions.

Will my site be mobile-friendly?

100%. Every site we build is fully responsive and tested across devices. Mobile-first design is standard, not an add-on.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

Yes. Hosting is included in all our plans. We can register a new domain for you or transfer your existing one. SSL certificates, CDN, and backups are all handled automatically.

Can I see analytics and performance data?

Absolutely. We integrate Google Analytics 4 and provide access to your dashboard. Web-as-a-Service clients also get monthly reports with traffic, conversions, and optimization recommendations.

What if I already have a website?

We can rebuild it from scratch or migrate/redesign your existing site. We'll preserve your SEO rankings and redirect old URLs to maintain your search presence.

Gideon & AI Features

What is Gideon?

Gideon is our dual AI co-pilot (Claude + GPT-4) that lives inside every project. It helps with strategy, monitors builds for issues, suggests optimizations, and can even interact with your site visitors through the AI chatbot add-on.

How much does the Gideon AI Chatbot cost?

Basic plan is $99/mo + $497 setup. Professional plan is $299/mo + $997 setup. It includes custom training on your business, lead capture, appointment booking, and 24/7 visitor support.

Can Gideon help me choose the right plan?

Yes! Use our Plan Selector tool where Gideon asks you 5 questions about your business and goals, then returns a custom recommendation with pricing, timeline, and suggested add-ons. It takes 2 minutes.

CRM & Account Executive Program

What is the Gideon CRM?

It's a production-grade, multi-tenant CRM we built in 4 weeks with 12 AI features, 31 industry-specific templates (more than Salesforce's 16), and a white-label AI concierge system. You can buy it outright or rent it monthly.

How does the Account Executive program work?

You bring the relationships and close deals. We deliver world-class product and handle all fulfillment. You earn recurring commissions, own your book of business, and can scale by recruiting and mentoring other AEs. No overhead, no product risk—just performance-based income.

Do I need sales experience to join as an AE?

We prefer sales experience, but hunger and coachability matter more. If you have relationships with business owners and can articulate value, we'll train you on our process and product stack.
