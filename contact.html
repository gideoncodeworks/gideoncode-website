Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed and launched within 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity and how quickly you provide content and feedback.

What's the difference between one-time and monthly plans?

One-time builds have higher upfront costs and limited post-launch support. Monthly plans have lower setup fees, include hosting, and provide ongoing updates, security, and support for as long as you're subscribed.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes! Our monthly plans are flexible. You can upgrade to add features or downgrade if your needs change. Just reach out to discuss your options.

Do you provide content writing?

We can help with basic copywriting and content organization, but for best results, you should provide the core content about your business. We'll help polish and optimize it.

What if I need help after my site launches?

Monthly plan customers get ongoing support included. One-time build customers receive 30-90 days of support (depending on tier), after which we can provide support on an hourly basis or you can upgrade to a monthly plan.

Can you help with SEO and marketing?

Absolutely! All our sites include basic SEO optimization. We also offer Social Media Management ($250/mo) and Google SEO/Ads Management ($297/mo) as add-on services.
